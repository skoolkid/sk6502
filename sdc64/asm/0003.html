$0003: Teachers' safe combination letters
This corresponds to $7FA3 in the ZX Spectrum version.
Used by the routines at $292F and $298B. The safe combination itself is stored at $90.
$0003 .BYTE $00 MR WACKER's safe combination letter is stored here.
$0004 .BYTE $00 MR ROCKITT's safe combination letter is stored here.
$0005 .BYTE $00 MR WITHIT's safe combination letter is stored here.
$0006 .BYTE $00 MR CREAK's safe combination letter is stored here.
