$0040: MSB of the lesson clock
This corresponds to $7FF7 in the ZX Spectrum version.
Used by the routines at $0800, $0C31, $0EC2, $149F, $1D4A, $277D, $28BC, $2BB0, $2D47 and $3AD5. The LSB of the lesson clock is at $FD.
