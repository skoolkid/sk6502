$0042: Temporary variables
$0042 .BYTE $00 Used by the routines at $3AD5 and $3B46 to store the current lesson identifier.
$0043 .BYTE $00 Used by the routine at $3AD5 to form the MSB of the address of a character's personal timetable entry. The LSB is the lesson identifier at $42 above.
$0044 .BYTE $00 The value $44 is placed here by the routine at $2D47 but never used.
