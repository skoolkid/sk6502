$8CE0: Lesson descriptors
This corresponds to $ACE0 in the ZX Spectrum version.
Used by the routine at $3AD5.
Each entry in this table defines the teacher for the corresponding period (if any), and the room. The teacher is identified by bits 4-7:
Bits Teacher
0000 MR WACKER
0001 MR ROCKITT
0010 MR WITHIT
0011 MR CREAK
0100 None
The room is identified by bits 0-3:
Bits Room
0001 READING ROOM
0010 MAP ROOM
0011 WHITE ROOM
0100 EXAM ROOM
0101 REVISION LIBRARY
0110 DINNER
0111 PLAYTIME
