$16DF: 'H' pressed - hit
This corresponds to $F492 in the ZX Spectrum version.
Called from the main loop at $0800.
$16DF LDX #$04 Bit 2 set: ERIC is hitting.
$16E1 LDY #$0A $0A is the animatory state of ERIC raising his fist.
$16E3 LDA #$12 This will initialise ERIC's action timer at $FE to $12.
$16E5 JMP $16B1 Start the punch.
