$002C: Sound effect identifier
Used by the routines at $189D, $2BB0, $2D47, $3AD5 and $BD00.
Holds one of the following values to indicate the sound effect currently being played:
Value Sound effect
0 None
1 A shield has been hit
2 ERIC is jumping
3 Bell is ringing
