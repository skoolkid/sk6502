$40E5: Command list $8A: Do nothing
This corresponds to $FBED in the ZX Spectrum version.
This command list appears in the timetables for BOY WONDER's and ERIC's pellets (see $8AE0 and $8BE0) but is not used by them. Command list $88 also continues here.
$40E5 .WORD $0A57 Do nothing.
