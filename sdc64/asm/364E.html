$364E: Turn ERIC round
This corresponds to $656A in the ZX Spectrum version.
Used by the routines at $34B5, $350B, $3558 and $359E.
$364E JSR $322E Update the SRB for ERIC's current animatory state and location.
$3651 LDA $26 Flip bit 7 of ERIC's animatory state at $26, thus turning him round.
$3653 EOR #$80
$3655 STA $26
$3657 LDA #$05 Store the value 5 at $13, where it will remain unused.
$3659 STA $13
$365B RTS
